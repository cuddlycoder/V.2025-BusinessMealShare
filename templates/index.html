OUR GOAL

MealShare is a website that helps businesses work together to fight hunger. It connects companies that want to help provide food with businesses like grocery stores. By working together, they can make a big difference. MealShare’s goal is to bring these two groups together to help reduce hunger.


HOW IT WORKS

The Donate button is for organizations that distribute food to society for a price and may have leftovers. Examples include grocery stores. They will fill out a form and contact the receiver. The Receive button is for non-profit organizations that distribute food to the needy. They can choose what they would like to receive and which grocery store. Accordingly, the grocery stores will give the food to the organizations which will then be distributed to the needy.
